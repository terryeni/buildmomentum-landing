Why this exists

I quit the gym four times. It was never motivation.

Every tracker I used had a daily streak. One rest day → streak gone → “well, I've ruined it” → quit by week 3. The streak that was supposed to motivate me was the exact thing making me quit.

The fix was stupidly simple: count consistency by the week, not the day. Hit your sessions for the week — rest days are part of the plan. And stop deciding what to train. Pre-decide, then just start.

I built Momentum around those two ideas because nothing else did both. It's still working.
